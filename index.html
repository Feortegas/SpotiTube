    SpotiTube Tutorial

    Welcome to Spotitube Tutorial

    SpotiTube is a web app that will allow you to search for a Spotify Playlist, then searches in YouTube for Official Music Videos of the Tracks in the Playlist. You will have the option to Automatically create a YouTube Playlist and add those Music Videos to your Playlist (requires Google Authentication). You can also add the Music Videos to an existing Playlist of your choice.

    1. Search for a Spotify Playlist
    2. SpotiTube will display all the Tracks on the Spotify Playlist and below it, all the YouTube Official Music Videos found
    3. Now you have the option to login to your Google Account and allow SpotiTube to create a Playlist Automatically, or add the Music Videos to an existing Playlist.
    4. Repeat with another Playlist search
